About

Delight Cake Shop is a very popular cake producing business started in year 2010 we are based in South B shopping center and our bakery has two others branches located in Nairobi and Thika.Our shops are regularly featured in local a media. The DelightCake Shop is a focal point for many of the guided walking people that pass through the city as well as providing locals with an opportunity to watch our skilled cake artists at work. The Cake Shop sells a wide range of cake decorating accessories including edible goods, sugarcraft materials.
